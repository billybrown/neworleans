Download as a .eps, .ai or .png file.

As reference for this collection I used the archive of photographs on the flickr page of the Preservation Resource Center of New Orleans. They advocate for unique and historic buildings that are due to be demo'd and I highly recommend throwing them a few bucks if you dig this project.
